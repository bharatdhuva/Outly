# Cold Mail Guide for JobOS (Internship Focused) - 2026

**Author:** Bharat Dhuva (JobOS)
**Version:** 1.0
**Focus:** Internship applications (learn + contribute)

Yeh guide tere JobOS Cold Mail Manager ke liye bana hai. AI prompt mein isko directly use kar sakta hai.

---

## 1. Subject Line Best Practices

- Length: 3-10 words (6-8 words ideal)
- Personalized + recent company hook wale subjects -> **50% higher open rates**
- Ultra-short subjects bhi bahut kaam karte hain

**Best Subject Patterns:**
- `[Name], loved your [recent thing]`
- `Quick question about [specific project]`
- `[City] [Role] intern here - 10 min chat?`
- `Saw your recent [post/integration] from Vadodara`

**Resilient Tech ke liye Recommended Subjects:**
1. `Sagar, loved your RazorpayX + ERPNext integration`
2. `Quick question about your banking integration`
3. `Vadodara CS student seeking ERPNext internship`

---

## 2. Email Body Rules

- **Length:** 5-8 lines maximum (under 120 words)
- **Structure:**
  1. Line 1 -> Strong personalization hook (recent LinkedIn post, feature, integration)
  2. Line 2-3 -> Who you are + relevant skills
  3. Line 4 -> Why them + learning intent (internship ke liye)
  4. Last line -> Clear ask (10-minute call)

- **Tone:** Warm, genuine, humble & confident. No buzzwords.

---

## 3. Personalization Tips

- Har email mein company ka **1 specific recent activity** mention karo (LinkedIn post, blog, new feature, client, GitHub).
- Vadodara local touch add kar sakta hai (chhoti companies mein effective hai).
- User ko `personalization_hook` field mein daalne ko bolo.

---

## 4. Best Practices & Timing

- Best send time: Subah 7-10 AM
- Follow-up: 3-4 din baad (short message)
- Attachment: Resume PDF + LinkedIn profile
- Reply rate target: 15-25% with good personalization

**Avoid:**
- Generic templates
- Long emails
- Words like "passionate", "excited", "opportunity"
- Typos in subject or body

---

## 5. Final Example Cold Mail (Resilient Tech - Internship)

**Subject:**
Sagar, loved your RazorpayX + ERPNext integration

**Email Body:**

Hi Sagar,
I came across your recent LinkedIn post on the RazorpayX + ERPNext banking integration - the one-click bulk payments and smart invoice flow looks really clean and useful for SMBs.
I'm Bharat Dhuva, a 3rd-year Computer Science student from Vadodara. I've been working hands-on with Python, Frappe Framework, and building custom ERPNext apps.
I'd love to join your team as an intern - learn from real projects and contribute to banking or India Compliance features while gaining practical experience.
Would you be open to a quick 10-minute call this week if there's any fit?
Thanks for your time!
Best,
Bharat Dhuva
Vadodara, Gujarat
