# Cold Mail Guide for Outly (Internship Focused) - 2026

**Author:** Bharat Dhuva (Outly)
**Version:** 1.0
**Focus:** Internship applications (learn + contribute)

Yeh guide tere Outly Cold Mail Manager ke liye bana hai. AI prompt mein isko directly use kar sakta hai.

---

## 1. Subject Line Best Practices

- Length: 3-10 words (6-8 words ideal)
- Personalized + recent company hook wale subjects -> **50% higher open rates**
- Ultra-short subjects bhi bahut kaam karte hain

**Best Subject Patterns:**
- `[Name], loved your [recent thing]`
- `Quick question about [specific project]`
- `[City] [Role] intern here - 10 min chat?`

---

## 2. Email Body Rules

- **Length:** 5-8 lines maximum (under 120 words)
- **Structure:**
  1. Line 1 -> Strong personalization hook (recent company update or feature)
  2. Line 2-3 -> Candidate background + relevant skills (from user settings)
  3. Line 4 -> Value proposition & role interest
  4. Last line -> Clear call-to-action (10-minute call request)

- **Tone:** Warm, genuine, professional & concise.

---

## 3. Personalization & Guidelines

- Reference 1 specific activity or tech detail from the target company context.
- Use candidate skills and target details strictly loaded from user profile settings.

---

## 4. Best Practices & Timing

- Best send time: Morning 7-10 AM
- Follow-up: 3-4 days after initial email
- Attachment: Resume PDF + LinkedIn profile

**Avoid:**
- Generic spam templates
- Long walls of text
- Typos in recipient names or company details

---

## 5. Standard Cold Mail Format

**Subject Pattern:**
`{{RecipientName}}, loved your {{RecentProject}}`

**Email Body Pattern:**

Hi {{RecipientName}},

I came across your recent update regarding {{RecentProject}} — the implementation looks really clean and impressive.

I'm {{SenderName}}, {{SenderBackground}}. I've been working hands-on with {{SenderSkills}}.

I'd love to contribute to your team as a {{TargetRole}} — learning from your engineering team while delivering tangible value on active projects.

I've attached my resume for your reference. Would you be open to a quick 10-minute call next week to explore potential alignment?

Best regards,
{{SenderName}}
{{SenderLocation}}
