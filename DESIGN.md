## Brand & Style
The design system replicates an exceptionally premium, editorial, and structured **Warm-Minimalist Landing Page** style. The brand personality is **Candid, Structured, and Calm**, presenting complex automation tools through highly readable, spacious, and elegant layout blocks.

Visual highlights include:
- **Warm Canvas:** A soft cream/beige background (#FCFAF7) replacing cold whites and dark blues to create a calm, approachable interface.
- **Editorial Typography:** Pairing a striking, classical serif headline font (Playfair Display) with a highly legible, modern sans-serif body font (Inter). The signature style uses italicized, low-contrast primary colored words for main punchlines (e.g., *sorted.*, *actual work.*).
- **Ruby Crimson Accents:** A high-contrast ruby-magenta primary color (#D31342) used for actions, highlights, and critical links.
- **Clean Structure:** 1px borders (#E6E2DA) enclosing white card blocks, accompanied by soft, flat drop shadows.

## Component-Level Rules

### Buttons
- **Primary:** Solid ruby-magenta (#D31342) with white text. High padding, rounded corners (8px), and a subtle hover lift.
- **Secondary:** Transparent outline with thin gray borders, utilizing dark text and a subtle hover background fill.

### Cards & Layouts
- **Dark Highlight Block:** A wide, rounded card in deep charcoal (#23211F) featuring white text, clean line items, and warm amber accents.
- **Feature Grid:** Highly structured white cards with clean line illustrations, structured copy, and minimal spacing.

### Forms & Input
- Light warm-white background, thin border, and sharp focus ring in ruby-magenta.
